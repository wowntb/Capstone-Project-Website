I've watched over 30 anime. Some good, some bad. A useful tool for keeping track of them, rating them, and finding something new to watch is a site called MyAnimeList. To do the above mentioned the user just has to create a free account and add titles to their list. The user can also keep track of their manga (comics) separately.

Users can post reviews and make their anime and manga lists public to other users. Here is MAL profile if you'd like to see which anime I've watched. You can see things such as my favourite characters and titles, and what I'm currently watching this season.

Alternatives to MAL:

  • AniList
  • Anime-Planet
  • IMDb (not specific to anime)
